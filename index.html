A DeFi-powered game economy where NFTs have real value

  • Participate in a standalone, location-dependent economy where every in-game NFT item is collateralized by tokens from the VITAL Index.
  • Have an easier time trading NFTs that contain REAL monetary value and are not just sentiment-driven.
  • Learn and level up Talents, participate in Crafting Collectives, and earn fees whenever NFTs are crafted.

Rogue-like survival in a dystopian world

  • Go solo or survive with friends in a co-op instance.
  • Explore locations that flucuate in resources and missions, determined through player activity.
  • Manage your Health and Stamina while achieving mission objectives to win.
  • Collect character avatars, uncover secrets, and achieve world outcomes as a community.

Fast-paced, action-RPG style combat

  • Classless characters give you the flexibility to build your combat specialization based on what you equip.
  • Buy or craft your weapon, armor, and accessory NFTs.
  • Battle through a variety of game modes: Hero Defense, PVE, Territory Expansion, Faction Wars, and Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA).
  • Mash those hotkeys with our novel combo chaining control system to rain death on your foes.

Earn from the land you own

  • Own in-game land NFTs that serve as yield optimizers while you explore the world.
  • Obtain equippable land enhancement NFTs that help improve yields and build up your land net worth.
  • Get land valued, trade it, stake it, or take up mortgage loans to unlock funds for your other investments.

An immersive, built-from-scratch narrative

  • Enjoy your time in the Timeleap world with a narrative that includes humor, satire, and a dash of pop culture.
  • Unravel mysteries, discover hidden plots, and learn about the origins of Timeleap Industries across seasons in Book 1.
  • Explore the world to help rebuild the facilities, while unlocking side stories as a community.
